How it works
Local-first

Your files live on your machine. No accounts required to use the apps. No syncing unless you want it.

One design language

All four apps share the same palette, type system, and interaction patterns. Open any of them and you're already home.

Each tool does one thing

Seed captures. Scene Board structures. Provenance documents. Works presents. No feature creep between them.
