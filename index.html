What is Procestra

Procestra is a suite of four native Mac apps built around how creative work actually gets made: Seed for ideas, Scene Board for structure, Provenance for process, Works for the finished result.

Local-first. No accounts. No subscriptions you can't escape from. No surveillance. Built for makers who care about the long, careful kind of work — and want their tools to reflect that.

The apps
Seed
Where ideas take root.
A garden for ideas. Plant them. Let them grow. Transplant the ones that take.
Scene Board
Structure before you shoot.
Where stories find their shape. Beats, scenes, structure — flexible, visual, yours.
Provenance
The work remembers.
The quiet ledger of how a project gets made. Every save, every source, every paste — recorded.
Works
Present what you've made.
Your portfolio, on your terms. Local-first. Polished without polish you didn't ask for.
