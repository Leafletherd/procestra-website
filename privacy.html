Privacy Policy

Effective May 2026

The short version

Procestra apps store your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your creative work. The website collects your email address only if you choose to join the beta waitlist.

The apps

Seed, Scene Board, Provenance, and Works are local-first applications. Your files — ideas, notes, boards, history, portfolios — are written to disk on your machine and nowhere else. No account is required to use any Procestra app. No telemetry is sent without your explicit opt-in.

The website

If you submit your email address via the beta signup form on this site, we store it for the purpose of notifying you when beta access opens. We use Netlify to host this site and process form submissions. Netlify's own privacy policy applies to that data.

We will not share your email address with third parties, send you marketing unrelated to Procestra, or contact you after beta communication is complete without your consent.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not use cookies. We do not currently run any third-party analytics on this site. If that changes, this policy will be updated and visitors will be notified.

Data retention

Beta waitlist email addresses are retained until beta access has been distributed, after which they are deleted. You may request removal at any time by emailing hello@procestra.app.

Children

Procestra apps and this website are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the site or apps after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at hello@procestra.app.
